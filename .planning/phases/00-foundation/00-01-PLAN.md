| T-00-01-03 | Information Disclosure | Hard-coded fallback secrets in code paths | mitigate | `APP_SECRET_KEY` and `APP_ENCRYPTION_KEY` have NO defaults in `Settings`; missing env vars raise at startup. `extra="forbid"` in `SettingsConfigDict` rejects unknown env keys (defense in depth against rogue overrides). |
