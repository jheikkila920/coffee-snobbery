Close the cost-control and correctness warnings from 19-REVIEW.md that touch the
AI quota / prediction / error-handling paths. These weaken the non-negotiable AI
cost controls or hide real failures; none is an XSS sink (those are 19-08).

- WR-02: `get_or_refresh_prediction` flushes a prediction row on the cache-hit
  path but `generate_coffee_research` never commits there. `get_session`
  (app/dependencies/db.py:61-70) does NOT commit on teardown — it closes/rolls

  recomputed on the next hit, defeating the 7-day TTL and burning LLM calls.

  even on the cache-hit path, writing no telemetry row and counting against no
  quota bucket — an implicit, unbounded cost bypass. Make the choice explicit.

  `except Exception` at :1114, :1126) make the specific entries dead and swallow
  programming errors; narrow them so unexpected exceptions propagate.
- WR-05/IN-02: the "delta → Hh Mm" math is copy-pasted in ~6 sites with
  inconsistent clamping (negative-countdown bug) plus an `__import__("datetime")`
  hack. Extract one `format_reset` helper in ai_quota.py and replace all sites.
- WR-01: document the rolling-24h quota TOCTOU window as accepted-risk (do NOT
  implement the advisory-lock-before-quota-read fix).

stop hiding real provider/programming failures.
